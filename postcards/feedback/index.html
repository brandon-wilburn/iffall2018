Postcard Feedback for Sarah

Aesthetics

  • The design works. It isn't necessarily remarkable though.
  • Images and text do support the overall content of the cards
  • There is evident cohesion among the postcards
  • The text is legible, however in some moments it becomes slightly harder to read

Structure

  • Perhaps your visual hierarchy isn't working the way you want. It's easy for me to get stuck in the background, but not for a long time.
  • The design elements are working together well. They could be improved, but they work.
  • The Jim quote/image may make it weird/hard to read on phone sizes because of how busy the foreground is.
  • You have an established structure for foreground/background with your slightly blurry backgrounds. This can be seen in the first and third mockups, but this rule is not as apparent in the second.

Technical Challenges

  • The text on all of the versions are fitting. But on the michael scott quote, the text on the blinds is a little bit too much.
  • The images are at a decent resolution, but could be better
